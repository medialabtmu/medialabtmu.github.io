Best Paper Award at IEEE ISM 2017

December 13, 2017 · 1 min read
news

Congratulations to Randy Tan! His paper on real-time human activity analysis won the Best Paper Award at the IEEE International Symposium on Multimedia (ISM) 2017, Workshop on Quality of Experience in Visual Media Processing and Transmission.

The work demonstrates a real-time system for recognizing and analyzing human activities from video, with applications in surveillance, healthcare monitoring, and interactive media.
