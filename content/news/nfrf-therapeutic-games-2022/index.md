Dr. Naimul Khan has been awarded a **$250,000 New Frontiers in Research Fund (NFRF) Exploration Award** to develop dynamic therapeutic VR experiences for children on the autism spectrum.

The project is a collaboration with [Wellwave Inc.](https://wellwave.ca/) and the [Holland Bloorview Kids Rehabilitation Hospital](https://hollandbloorview.ca/) — Canada's largest children's rehabilitation hospital. The research will focus on creating adaptive, personalized VR interventions that respond to real-time physiological and behavioural sensing to maximize therapeutic benefit.
