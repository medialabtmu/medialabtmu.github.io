Dr. Naimul Khan has been awarded a $90,000 NSERC Collaborative Research and Development Grant in partnership with [AWE Company Ltd.](https://www.awexr.com/)

This one-year project will develop machine learning algorithms that leverage point cloud data from modern mobile AR libraries (ARKit/ARCore) to provide intelligent 3D scene understanding. The resulting scene understanding capabilities will further enable **collaborative augmented reality experiences** between multiple users sharing the same physical space.
