Dr. Naimul Khan has been awarded a **$400,000 NSERC Alliance Grant** in partnership with [Wellwave Inc.](https://wellwave.ca/)

The project will focus on developing therapeutic virtual reality games with integrated multimodal stress assessment for children. By continuously sensing physiological and behavioural responses, the system adapts game content in real time to support stress regulation and therapeutic outcomes.
