Dr. Naimul Khan has been awarded an NSERC Alliance grant (with Mitacs Accelerate support) totalling $120K to develop AI algorithms for segmentation and analysis of intestinal ultrasound, in partnership with [Dova Health Intelligence](https://www.dovahealth.ca/).

The project targets faster and more accurate assessment of Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD) — a condition affecting millions of Canadians — by automating the interpretation of intestinal ultrasound images using deep learning. IBD assessment currently relies heavily on specialist expertise, and AI-assisted tools have the potential to significantly reduce diagnostic delays.

This project is led by Dr. Randy Tan as post-doctoral research lead.
