Congratulations to Randy Tan! His paper on real-time human activity analysis won the **Best Paper Award** at the [IEEE International Symposium on Multimedia (ISM) 2017](https://www.ieeesmc.org/), Workshop on Quality of Experience in Visual Media Processing and Transmission.

The work demonstrates a real-time system for recognizing and analyzing human activities from video, with applications in surveillance, healthcare monitoring, and interactive media.
