Dr. Naimul Khan has been awarded a $25,000 NSERC Engage grant in partnership with [Shaftesbury VR](https://shaftesbury.ca/).

The project will develop a multimodal stress assessment algorithm to be integrated into Shaftesbury's **Positive Distraction Entertainment System** — a platform designed to dynamically adapt VR game content to reduce anxiety in children preparing for medical procedures. By fusing physiological and behavioural signals, the system aims to provide a non-pharmacological approach to pre-procedural anxiety reduction.
