RML will be presenting at the **[IEEE International Symposium on Biomedical Imaging (ISBI) 2025](https://biomedicalimaging.org/2025/)**, showcasing the latest progress on AI-guided neonatal cardiac ultrasound analysis.

**Paper:** *Temporal Feature Weaving for Neonatal Echocardiographic Viewpoint Video Classification*
S. French, F. Zhu, A. Jain, N. Khan

The work introduces a novel temporal feature weaving approach for automatically classifying echocardiographic viewpoints in neonatal ultrasound video, a critical step toward fully automated cardiac assessment in the NICU.
