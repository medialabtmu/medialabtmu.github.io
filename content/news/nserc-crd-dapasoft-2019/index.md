Dr. Ling Guan and Dr. Naimul Khan have been awarded a $300,000 NSERC Collaborative Research and Development Grant in partnership with [Dapasoft Inc.](https://dapasoft.com/)

This two-year project will develop new information processing tools and techniques to enhance emergency medical services. The system will utilize IoT sensors to continuously record patient symptoms, with machine learning analysis and immersive context-aware visualization methods to assist healthcare professionals in timely diagnosis and decision-making in emergency situations.
