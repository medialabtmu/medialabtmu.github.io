RML members will present two papers at the **42nd Annual International Conference of the IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBC 2020)**.

Both papers report the latest results from the lab's ongoing collaboration with Shaftesbury Inc., focusing on stress assessment from multimodal physiological sensors using machine learning. The work explores fusion of ECG, EEG, and behavioural signals to build robust, real-time stress classifiers for VR-based therapeutic applications.
