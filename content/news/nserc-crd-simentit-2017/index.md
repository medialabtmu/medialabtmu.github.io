Dr. Naimul Khan, Dr. Ling Guan, and Dr. Andy Ye have been awarded a $375,000 NSERC Collaborative Research and Development Grant in partnership with [SimentIT Inc.](https://simentit.com/)

This two-year project will develop novel 3D object recognition and tracking algorithms for museum augmented reality exhibits. The technology will be deployed at the newly renovated [Canada Science and Technology Museum](https://ingeniumcanada.org/sciencetech) in Ottawa, enabling visitors to experience interactive AR overlays on physical artifacts.
