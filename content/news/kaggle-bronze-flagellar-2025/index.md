MASc student Yufei Ren and Dr. Naimul Khan won a **Bronze Medal** in the [Kaggle BYU Locating Bacterial Flagellar Motors in 3D Tomograms competition](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/byu-locating-bacterial-flagellar-motors-2025), placing **64th out of 1,136 teams**.

The task involved detecting bacterial flagellar motor locations in 3D cryo-electron tomograms — a challenging volumetric detection problem with significant implications for structural biology. Our approach was based on [MHAF-YOLO](https://github.com/yang-0201/MHAF-YOLO), a multi-head attention feature fusion architecture adapted for 3D detection.

Congratulations Yufei!
