Congratulations to **Julian True**! His paper [*"Motion Vector Extrapolation for Video Object Detection"*](/publications/true-2023-motion/) won the **Best Paper Runner-Up Award** at the CVPR 2021 Robust Video Understanding Workshop.

The paper demonstrates that leveraging motion vectors from compressed video can decrease object detection latency by up to **25×** while maintaining detection accuracy — a significant advance for real-time video understanding on resource-constrained devices.
