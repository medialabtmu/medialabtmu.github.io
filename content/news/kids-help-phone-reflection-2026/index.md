Kids Help Phone youth councillor **Simran** has written a reflection piece on RML's recent pilot project conducted in partnership with [Kids Help Phone](https://kidshelpphone.ca/) — exploring the development of an agentic AI framework for training frontline support workers.

The piece offers a youth perspective on how AI-assisted training tools could improve the consistency and quality of mental health support for young people.
