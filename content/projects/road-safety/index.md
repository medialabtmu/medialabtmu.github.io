## Overview

In partnership with Alcohol Countermeasure Systems, this project develops a novel cloud-based multimodal biometrics framework for continuous driver authentication and real-time drowsiness detection. By combining multiple physiological and behavioral signals, the system aims to significantly enhance road safety by identifying impaired or fatigued drivers before accidents occur.
